Winter Wonderland

Submitted by Barry
Tags slow decline, helplessness
Written about 1 year ago

Rise. Fall.

In the quiet field, what should have been a gentle, beautiful snowfall was an avalanche to the broken man. He had long since given up trying to move; choosing to recline comfortably and view his life as it passed. His arms were covered. His legs would be blanketed soon.

Rise. Fall.

As time slowed, he could hear each individual flake crashing into his body. Some melted and rolled away harmlessly. Most landed, stuck, and waited for a cascade of friends.

Rise.

The wind swept and the snow drifted.

Fall.
